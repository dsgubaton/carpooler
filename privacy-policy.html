Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

Introduction

Carpool Splitter ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Carpool Splitter collects and processes the following types of data:

Data You Provide:

  • Names: Person names you enter for carpool participants
  • Carpool Data: Car assignments, seating arrangements, and related information you create
  • Event Information: Trip details and carpool configurations

Automatically Collected Data:

  • Network Information: IP address and connection data (collected by Firebase for service functionality)
  • Device Identifiers: Anonymous identifiers used by Firebase for real-time sync functionality

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide real-time collaborative carpool planning
  • Sync data across devices participating in the same carpool event
  • Enable sharing of carpool arrangements via links
  • Maintain and improve app functionality

Data Storage and Security

Your carpool data is stored using Google Firebase Realtime Database. We implement the following security measures:

  • Data is stored on secure Firebase servers
  • Each carpool event uses a unique event ID
  • Event data is isolated and not shared between different carpool events
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes

Data Retention

Carpool event data is retained in Firebase until you choose to delete it. You can clear your carpool data at any time using the "Clear" button in the app.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your carpool data at any time
  • Delete your carpool data using the app's clear function
  • Stop using the app and discontinue data collection

Third-Party Services

Carpool Splitter uses Firebase (Google) for real-time database functionality. Firebase's privacy policy can be found at: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Children's Privacy

Carpool Splitter does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audience use in organizing carpool logistics.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the app's GitHub repository.
